Based on the data in the table, answer the questions 1-5:Programme-wise Percentage of Successful StudentsQuestion: In B. Com programme, 44% of the total students passed. If total number of boys in B. Com is 200, then what is the total number of girls in B. Com? (UGC NET 11 Jul 2022 Morning)
  • A.50
  • B.52
  • C.66
  • D.60
Correct Answer: 50

Let's assume that the total number of students in the B.Com. program is x.

Number of students who passed = 44% of x

Number of boys who passed = 40% of 200

Number of girls who passed = 60% of y

Since the total number of students who passed the program is the same as the number of boys and girls who passed, we can write:

44% of x = 40% of 200 + 60% of y

0.44x = 80 + 0.6y

We can also write an equation to relate the total number of students in the program to the number of boys and girls:

x = 200 + y

Now we have two equations with two variables. We can substitute the second equation into the first equation to get:

0.44 (200 + y) = 80 + 0.6y

Simplifying this equation, we get:

88 + 0.44y = 80 + 0.6y

0.16y = 8

y = 50

Therefore, the total number of girls in the B.Com. program is 50.
