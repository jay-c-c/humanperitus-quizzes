Question 1
Two numbers are in the ratio 2: 5. If 16 is added to both the numbers, their ratio becomes 1: 2. The numbers are:
  • A.16, 40
  • B.20, 50
  • C.28, 70
  • D.32, 80
Correct Answer: 32, 80

Let, the original numbers be 2x and 5x.

Now, 16 is added to both the numbers, hence, the numbers become 2x+16 and 5x+16 and their ratio becomes 1x:2x.

So, the equations become,

2x + 16 = x ......(1)

5x + 16 = 2x......(2)

Now, solving equation (1), we get,

2x – x = 16

Hence, x =16.

Thus, the numbers are 2x and 5x, which is 2 × 16 = 32 and 5 × 16 = 80.

Hence, the correct answer is 32, 80.
